OPEN THE DOOR!

Time capsule refers to a container or device that gathers and preserves representative objects of a particular era for the purpose of opening them up in the future. The first modern time capsule is cited as a "culture graveyard" buried at Oglesov University in Atlanta, the U.S. on May 28, 1940. The collection of "The Cemetery of Civilization", which was released in 8113 A.D., is as follows.
